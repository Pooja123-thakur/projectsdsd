Me My Family Hobbies Favorite Foods

My favorite foods are Maggi Noodles, broccoli, chicken fry, and salad. I love Maggi Noodles because of the sweet spicy taste. I also love how the noodles taste after they are cooked. I love broccoli with ranch or steamed broccoli. They taste amazing when you don't have the sour ones. I love the chicken fry my mom cooks. She adds so much taste that I feel like I don't chew the chicken, but I immediately swallow it. The chicken is so mouthwatering. I also love salad because of the taste of all the different types of vegetables mixed with ranch.
